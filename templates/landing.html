Learning made easy.

YouNote is a platform specifically designed to enhance your learning experience. It is a note-taking app that lets you take detailed notes that are in sync with a YouTube tutorial of your choice. Whether you're acquiring a new skill or studying for an exam, YouNote has you covered!

Sync Your Videos + Notes

With YouNote you can take notes while simultaneously watching a video tutorial. You no longer have to keep switching between your note and video tabs.

Timestamp

Time-Track your tutorial with YouNote's timestamp feature. With just a click of a button, you can bookmark specific sections in the video to revisit.

Search For A YouTube Video

YouNote makes it easy for you to search for a tutorial within its app. YouNote aims to utilize YouTube's plethora of educational videos to make learning as easy and accessible as possible.
